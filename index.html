About Me

My name is Anna Józefowicz, I live in Poznań and I am looking for a job as a Junior Web Developer.

Hello!

I have good knowledge of HTML5, CSS, Sass, JavaScript as well as jQuery and Bootstrap libraries. I know the basics of Git, PHP and MySQL.

The technology that I enjoy the most is React. Recently, I finished my first project in which I used, among others Redux and Styled Components.

For 4 years I worked as a graphic designer in a printing company, where I used Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop on a daily basis. During this time, I learned to work under time pressure. The specific nature of this industry is the unequal number of orders, which on the one hand taught me how to work under time pressure, and on the other - using free time to develop and look for new solutions.

One of my successes at that time was writing a script for Illustrator, which automated and accelerated the work of the group. I soon became convinced that in programming I like to find solutions to specific problems the most.

I like to learn and I am open to new challenges. I believe that solid and systematic work brings the best results.
